# How to open AgeTech Connect on your iPhone

**Send this to the person who will test the app on their iPhone.**

---

## If they sent you a LINK (Option 2 – real app)

1. Open the link they sent you (tap it in the message or email).
2. Tap **Install** (or “Install app”) when the page opens.
3. If your iPhone says “Untrusted Developer”:
   - Go to **Settings → General → VPN & Device Management**.
   - Under “Developer App,” tap the developer name.
   - Tap **Trust**.
   - Open the app again from your home screen.
4. Use the app like any other app. You don’t need to open the link again.

---

## If they sent you a LINK or QR code (Option 1 – quick try)

1. Install **Expo Go** from the App Store (search “Expo Go”).
2. Open the **link** they sent you in **Safari** (don’t open it inside Expo Go).
   - Or open your **Camera** app and scan the **QR code** they sent. When it says “Open in Expo Go,” tap **Open**.
3. The app will load inside Expo Go. Use it from there.

---

If something doesn’t work, tell the person who sent you the link and they can help.
